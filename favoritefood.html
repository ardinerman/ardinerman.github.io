    • My Food in Madison

    Since I began living in Madison, WI as a freshman, I've been pretty involved in exploring the food scene. There are so many options to choose from: Ian's, Tags, Taste of Sichuan, and even the food trucks. And while I do eat food from those places on a more regular basis, I have made it a point to see what Madison has to offer in terms of higher-end restaurants. The 5 restaurants listed below are my favorite in Madison. They may be a little pricey, but on a special occasion like a birthday or parent's weekend, they sure are fun to eat at!

    My Top 5 Favorite Restaurants:

    1. Bar Corallini

    2. Cento

    3. Eno Vino

    4. Tempest

    5. Everly
